6 Common Questions
100% Transparency

Is this survey anonymous?

100% Anonymous

Yes, completely. We don't collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any information that could identify you. Your responses are stored securely and will only be used in aggregate form for research purposes.

No Personal Data Secure Storage Aggregate Only

Who can take part in this study?

All LGBTQ+ Identities

Anyone who identifies as LGBTQ+ and currently lives in India can participate. This includes all sexual orientations, gender identities, and expressions within the LGBTQ+ spectrum.

All Identities India Residents Inclusive

How will the data be shared?

Open Research

Aggregated, anonymized results will be made publicly available through this website and open research platforms. Individual responses will never be shared. The data will be used for academic research, advocacy, and community awareness.

Public Results No Individual Data Open Access

Can I skip sensitive questions?

Your Choice

Absolutely. Every question is optional. You can skip any question that makes you uncomfortable or doesn't apply to your experience. Partial responses are still valuable and appreciated.

All Optional Your Comfort Partial OK

How long does the survey take?

10-15 Minutes

The survey typically takes 10-15 minutes to complete. You can take breaks and return to it later if needed. Your progress will be saved automatically.

Quick & Easy Auto Save Take Breaks

Is this affiliated with any government or institution?

Community-Led

No, this is a completely independent, community-led initiative. We are not affiliated with any government body, institution, or organization that could compromise your anonymity or safety.

Independent Community-Led No Affiliation

If you need more information or have concerns not covered here, please don't hesitate to reach out through our community channels.
